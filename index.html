The Stanley Quencher is a reusable stainless steel tumbler introduced by Stanley in 2016. It comes in various sizes, including 14, 20, 30, 40, and 64 fluid ounces, and features vacuum insulation to maintain beverage temperature, a removable straw, and multiple color options. The Quencher gained significant popularity through influencer marketing on social media platforms, particularly TikTok.The company's annual sales increased from $70 million in 2019 to $750 million in 2023, largely due to the Quencher's success. The Quencher's popularity is attributed to its practical features, such as effective insulation, stylish design, and the convenience of fitting into car cup holders.The Stanley Quencher tumbler is primarily made of stainless steel and plastic components.
